Pacific Alumni

An avid alumni and advocate for University of the Pacific.

From its small family of students and faculty to its amazing programs that offer industry experience, Pacific is a place that I will advocate for the rest of my life. I served as Pacific Ambassador introducing new students and families, and led orientation groups. As a growing professional in the tech industry, I represented ON.Lab for recruitment of Pacific's engineer students and I continue to reach out in ways that I hope will help the engineering department.

Growing Up

My roots lie in South Korea where I was born and raised. When I was in 3rd grade, my small family of 3 moved out to Chicago, IL. I will never forget the cold breeze that first greeted us, and perhaps it foreshadowed the hardships that we were about to face and endure. That didn't stop us from exploring the new country, however. We loved roadtrips and drove to various states all around East Coast listening to casette tapes of The Eagles, Billy Joel, Beegees, and the likes. Our adventures eventually led us to Los Angeles, CA and it has been our home to this day.
